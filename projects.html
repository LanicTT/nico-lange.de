Re-Naming Telekom MMS GmbH

Once in a lifetime: Renaming of a company within a corporate group. As part of the realignment of Telekom Deutschland, T-Systems MMS was to be renamed.

In the project, which consisted of the facets legal changes, IT adjustments and communication and external presentation, I was responsible for the internal communication and external presentation, as well as for the exchange of the three work streams.

“We are MMS! And we will remain MMS.
By switching to Deutsche Telekom AG, we are moving closer to the medium-sized business customers of Telekom Deutschland. Of course, the strategic realignment underscores our position within the Group. But above all, we are gaining strength to drive forward topics and projects with our customers and within the Telekom network.”

Backpacking.de - Your virtual Home @ your journey

Imagine you are in a small village and have no idea what to do there. The next time you pack your backpack and are looking for information, Backpacking.de will be your place to go. On this site you will find some information about countries and cities, as you know it from the travel guides for individual tourists. A big advantage of the portal is that travel reports of others who have been there before you are linked to the city information. Another advantage is that all information is kept up to date by the users. So if a hotel closes or a museum is particularly good, travelers can enter this in the portal. So that everyone knows that you are doing well during your trip, there is the possibility to write a diary, upload pictures, there is a forum for exchanging opinions and everything else that is needed for communication.
